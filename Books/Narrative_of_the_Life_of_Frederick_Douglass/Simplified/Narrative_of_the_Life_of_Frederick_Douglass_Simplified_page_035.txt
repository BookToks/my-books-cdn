Colonel Lloyd’s slaves would brag about how their master could buy and sell Jacob Jepson. 

Mr. Jepson’s slaves would brag about how their master could whip Colonel Lloyd. 

These arguments almost always ended in fights between the slaves, and the one who won the fight was seen as right. 

They believed that their masters’ power somehow gave them power too. 

Being a slave was already bad, but being a poor man’s slave was seen as even worse!
