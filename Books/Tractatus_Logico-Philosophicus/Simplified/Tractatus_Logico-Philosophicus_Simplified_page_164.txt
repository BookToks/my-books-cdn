6.54 My sentences explain things so that the person who understands me will realize at the end that they don’t really make sense. This happens when they use my sentences to go beyond what I’m saying. (It’s like climbing a ladder and then throwing it away after you’ve reached the top.) They have to get past these sentences to see the world the right way.
