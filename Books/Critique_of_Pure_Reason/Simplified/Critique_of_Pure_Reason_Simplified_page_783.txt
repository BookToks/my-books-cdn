Judgment means making a decision or forming an opinion.  

Juxtaposition is when you put two things side by side to compare them.  

Know means to be aware of something or to recognize it.  

Knowledge is what you learn or understand about a subject.  

A lapse is a temporary mistake or failure.  

Law is a rule that people must follow.  

Limitation means a restriction or something that stops you from doing more.  

Magnitude means how big or important something is.  

Major premise is the main idea or statement in an argument.  

Manifold means many or various.
