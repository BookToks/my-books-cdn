Other people who liked it also downloaded Plato’s Apology. The Apology is Plato’s version of a speech Socrates gave to defend himself. He was accused of corrupting young people, not believing in the gods, and creating new gods. “Apology” here means speaking up to explain or defend your beliefs or actions.

Plato’s The Republic is a conversation written around 380 BC. It is one of the most important books about philosophy and politics. In the conversation, Socrates and others talk about what justice means and ask if a person who is fair is happier than one who is not. They imagine a society ruled by philosopher-kings and protectors.

The Republic also talks about the role of philosophers, Plato’s idea of perfect Forms, the role of poetry, and the idea that the soul lives forever.

Bertrand Russell’s Mysticism and Logic and Other Essays has essays about philosophy, religion, science, and math.

Friedrich Wilhelm Nietzsche’s The Antichrist is more about criticizing Christianity than about the devil or evil. Nietzsche says that Christianity has hurt western civilization and should end. No matter what you believe, The Antichrist is an interesting book for philosophy.
