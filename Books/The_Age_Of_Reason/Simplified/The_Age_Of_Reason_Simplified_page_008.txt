3 Chapter About Who Jesus Christ Was and His Story

Nothing here is meant to be rude about who Jesus Christ really was. He was a good and kind man. The way he taught people to live and act was very caring and kind. Even though others like Confucius, some Greek thinkers, the Quakers, and many good people in history taught similar ideas, his way of living was the best.

Jesus Christ didn’t write anything about himself—nothing about his birth, family, or life. None of the New Testament was written by him. Other people wrote the story of his life. The parts about him coming back to life and going up to heaven were added because, since the story said he was born in a special way, the writers had to say he left the world in a special way too, or the first part wouldn’t make sense.

The way the story about him coming back to life and going up to heaven is told is worse than the first part. The story about his special birth couldn’t be proven or shown to others, so people couldn’t check or prove it wrong. Jesus couldn’t prove it himself.
