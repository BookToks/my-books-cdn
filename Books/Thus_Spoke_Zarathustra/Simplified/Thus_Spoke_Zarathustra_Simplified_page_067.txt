After this, Zarathustra went back to the mountains and stayed alone in his cave. He stayed away from people, waiting like a farmer who has planted seeds. But his soul was restless and missed the people he cared about because he still had a lot to give them. The hardest thing is to stop giving even when you love someone and to stay humble as a giver.

So, the lonely man spent many months and years this way. During that time, his wisdom grew, but it also made him feel pain because he knew so much.

One morning, before the sun came up, he woke from a dream. After thinking quietly for a long time, he finally asked his heart, "Why did I wake up so suddenly from my dream?"
