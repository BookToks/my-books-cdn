Thus Spoke Zarathustra by Friedrich Nietzsche says, “Become who you are!” It is a book called Thus Spake Zarathustra: A Book for All and None.

The book includes different parts like the Prologue, First Part, Second Part, Third Part, and Fourth Part.

There are also extra sections like a timeline of Nietzsche’s life, a biography, his thoughts about his books, and an idea called Eternal Recurrence.

This version is based on the Thomas Common Translation but changed a lot by Bill Chapko.
