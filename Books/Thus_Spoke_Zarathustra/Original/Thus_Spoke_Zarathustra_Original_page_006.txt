(Or else as a web page at www.eternal-recurrence.com) Bill Chapko Editor Profile Born in 1947, Bill Chapko grew up in suburban New Jersey. At New York University he studied Existential Philosophy under Professor William Barrett, author of the book "Irrational Man: A Study in Existential Philosophy". He graduated with a BA in Philosophy in 1969. He has read, enjoyed and studied Nietzsche all his life.
 
 Since 1981 he, together with his American wife Eva and their four children, have lived happily in southern Italy.
