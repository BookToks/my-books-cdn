 And now , reader , I come to a period in my unhappy life , which I would gladly forget if I could . The re membrance fills me with sorrow and shame. It pains me to tell you of it ; but I have promised to tell you the truth , and I will do it honestly , let it cost me what it may I will not try to screen myself behind the plea of compulsion from a master ; for it was not so. Neither can I plead ignorance or thought lessness. For years, my master had done his utmost to pollute my mind with foul images, and to destroy the pure principles inculcated by my grandmother , and the good mistress of my childhood . The influ ences of slavery had had the same effect on me that they had on other young girls ; they had made me prematurely knowing , concerning the evil ways of the world . I knew what I did, and I did it with de liberate calculation .
