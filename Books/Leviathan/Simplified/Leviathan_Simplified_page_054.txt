Having servants is power; having friends is power too, because together they make you stronger.

Money combined with generosity is power because it helps you get friends and servants. Without generosity, money can cause people to be jealous instead of helping you.

Having a reputation for power is power because it attracts people who want protection.

Being known for loving your country, called popularity, is power for the same reason.

Any quality that makes a person liked or feared by many, or being known for that quality, is power because it helps you get support and help from others.

Success is power because it makes people think you are wise or lucky, so they either fear or trust you.

Being friendly to people already in power increases your power because it earns their love.

Being known as smart in handling peace or war is power because wise people are trusted to lead more than others.

Nobility is power, but only in places where nobles have special privileges; those privileges are their power.

Being good at speaking is power because it looks like wisdom.

Good looks are power because they promise good things and make others, like women and strangers, like you.

Knowledge is a small kind of power because it’s not obvious and only a few people have it or understand it well.

Skills used in public, like building defenses or making war machines, are power because they help protect and win battles. Even though science is the real source, people usually give credit to the workers who make these things.

A person's value is like the price others would pay to use their power. It’s not fixed but depends on what others need and think.
