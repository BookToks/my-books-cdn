CHAPTER XV THE VALUE OF PHILOSOPHY

Now that we have finished looking at some of the main problems in philosophy, it’s a good time to think about why philosophy is important and why people should study it. This is especially important because many people, influenced by science or everyday life, think philosophy is just pointless arguing about small details or questions that can’t be answered.

This idea about philosophy comes partly from misunderstanding what life is really about and partly from not understanding what philosophy is trying to achieve.
