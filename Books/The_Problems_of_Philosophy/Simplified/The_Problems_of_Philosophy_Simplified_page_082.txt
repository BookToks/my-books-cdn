I don’t mean that people don’t know they exist, but that they have never realized they have feelings and sensations. Because of this, they don’t realize that they, as the ones feeling these things, exist.

We said before that knowing what’s in our minds is called self-consciousness. But this isn’t really knowing ourselves; it’s just knowing certain thoughts and feelings. Whether we truly know our basic self, not just the thoughts and feelings, is a tough question and hard to answer for sure.

When we try to look inside ourselves, we always find some specific thought or feeling, not the “I” that has those thoughts or feelings. Still, there are some reasons to believe we do know this “I,” even though it’s hard to separate from everything else. To understand this better, let’s think about what it really means to know a particular thought.
