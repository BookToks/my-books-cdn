We know that the man with the iron mask was real, and many ideas have been suggested about him. But we don’t know exactly who he was.

We know that the person who gets the most votes will win. In this case, we probably know who that person is in some way. But we don’t know which candidate it actually is. In other words, we don’t know any statement like "A is the candidate who will get the most votes," where A is the name of a candidate.

We say we have "just descriptive knowledge" of someone when we know they exist, and maybe we know the actual person, but we don’t know any statement like "a is that person," where a is something we recognize.

When we say "the so-and-so exists," we mean there is exactly one person or thing who is that so-and-so.
