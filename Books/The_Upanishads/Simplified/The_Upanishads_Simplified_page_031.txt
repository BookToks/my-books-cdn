30 The Self is everywhere, shining bright, without a body or flaws. It has no muscles, is pure, never makes mistakes, sees and knows everything, is beyond this world, and exists on its own. It has arranged everything properly for all time.

This text explains what the Self really is. When our mind is cleaned from the stuff that weighs it down, only then can we see the huge, glowing, delicate, always pure and flawless Self, which is the true reason we exist.

They go into total darkness if they worship ignorance and confusion. They fall into even deeper darkness if they worship knowledge alone.

Knowledge gets you one result; ignorance gets you another. This is what wise people have said.

The person who understands both knowledge and ignorance can get past death through ignorance and reach eternal life through knowledge.
