Who would want to live a long life if they live in a world that is falling apart, but have found the immortal, unchanging truth?  
If someone thinks about how people enjoy beauty and pleasure, would they really want to just keep living longer?

Oh Death, you are something people don’t understand for sure.  
Tell us about what happens after we die.

Nachiketas doesn’t ask for anything else.  
He only wants to know this secret that is hidden.
