They asked Fire, "Hey Jataveda (All-Knowing), can you find out what this mysterious spirit is?" Fire said, "Yes."

He ran toward it, and Brahman asked him, "Who are you?" Fire replied, "I am Agni, I am Jataveda."

Brahman asked, "What power do you have?" Agni said, "I can burn everything on Earth."

Brahman gave him a straw and said, "Burn this." Agni rushed to burn it but couldn’t. He came back and told the gods, "I couldn’t figure out what this great mystery is."
