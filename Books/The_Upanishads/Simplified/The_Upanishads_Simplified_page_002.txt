THE UPANISHADS BY SWAMI PARAMANANDA My Belief.

I believe that the soul inside me is pure and never changes. It is the real me, not my body or mind.

I think that everything in the world is connected and comes from the same source.

I believe that we should be kind and honest because that helps our soul grow.

I know that learning and understanding the truth is very important for a happy life.

I believe that by being calm and loving, we can find peace inside ourselves.
