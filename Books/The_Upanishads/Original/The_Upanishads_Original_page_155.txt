154 The Upanishads XI THE sage Angiras in ancient times taught this truth (to Saunaka). It should not be studied by one who has not fulfilled the vow of self-sacrifice and service. Adoration to the great Rishis ! Adoration to the great Rishis !
 
 Here ends the Mundaka-Upanishad.
