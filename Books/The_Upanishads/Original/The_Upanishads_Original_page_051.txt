5o The Upanishads XXVIII WHAT man dwelling on the decaying mortal plane, having approached the undecaying immortal one, and having reflected upon the nature of enjoyment through beauty and sense pleasure, would delight in long life?
 
 XXIX O DEATH, that regarding which there is doubt, of the great Hereafter, tell us.
 
 Nachiketas asks for no other boon than that which penetrates this hidden secret.
