In order to acquire knowledge (of the Eternal) let him then, with sacrificial fuel in his hand, approach a Guru (spiritual teacher) who is well-versed in the Vedas (Scriptures) and who is establishd in Brahman (the Supreme).
 
 XIII TO him who has thus approached reverently, whose heart is tranquillized, and whose senses are under control, let the wise Guru teach the real knowledge of Brahman, by which the true and immortal Being is known.
