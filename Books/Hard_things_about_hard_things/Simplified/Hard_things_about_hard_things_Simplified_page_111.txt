Even though a lot of people help out, the final decision has to be made by one person alone. Only the CEO knows everything about what’s needed, why those things matter, all the feedback from interviews and references, and how important each person’s opinion is.

When a group tries to decide about top bosses together, they usually avoid picking someone really strong and instead pick someone who just doesn’t have big problems. It’s a tough and lonely job, but someone has to do it.
