Note on the Text xxvii The changes would thus seem to be intended in the main to avoid any implication of anti-Semitism. Although Du Bois continued to deny any intentional prejudice, it is noteworthy that, both in the letter to Aptheker and the memo to Blue Heron, he is expressly critical of the unrecognized or ‘unconscious’ bias in his original phrasing in the 1903 edition.
