My Inventions is Nikola Tesla’s autobiography. When he was 63 years old, Tesla wrote about his creative life.

It was first published in 1919 in a magazine called Electrical Experimenter.

Here is what’s inside:
I. My Early Life  
II. My First Efforts At Invention  
III. My Later Endeavors  
IV. The Discovery of the Tesla Coil and Transformer  
V. The Magnifying Transmitter  
VI. The Art of Telautomatics
