More! less! not sufficiently! so far! not altogether! What clearness and precision of ideas and language! And such eclectic professorial twaddle is modestly baptised by Mr. Roscher, “the anatomico-physiological method” of Political Economy! One discovery however, he must have credit for, namely, that money is “a pleasant commodity.” Chapter 3: Money, Or the Circulation of Commodities Section 1: The Measure of Values Throughout this work, I assume, for the sake of simplicity, gold as the money-commodity.
