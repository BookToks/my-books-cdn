Governor Shirley realized he had taken on those debts for the government’s work, so he made plans to pay them off and freed Franklin from his unpleasant position.

After Braddock’s defeat, people in the colonies were very scared.

Everyone started getting ready to fight.

In Pennsylvania, the Quakers’ influence stopped any defense plan that would force people to carry weapons. Franklin suggested a bill to create a militia where every man could choose if he wanted to carry arms or not.

The Quakers were okay with this because it let them stay peaceful while their neighbors could fight for them. Because of this law, a strong militia was formed. The fear of danger made everyone who wasn’t against war feel ready to fight. Franklin was made a colonel of a militia.
