I worked sometimes as a printer and sometimes at the press.

I only drank water. About fifty other workers drank a lot of beer. Sometimes, I carried a big stack of letters in each hand up and down the stairs, while the others used both hands to carry just one. They were surprised to see that I, called the American Aquatic, was stronger than those who drank beer.

The beer-boy was busy all day serving beer to that place. My friend at the press drank a pint of beer before breakfast, a pint with bread and cheese for breakfast, one between breakfast and lunch, one around six in the evening, and another after work. I thought this was awful, but he said he needed all that beer to get strength for working.
