Great Britain got so many benefits from its colonies that only someone really crazy could keep doing things that made the colonies feel unhappy and want to break away. 

When we think about how much better government ideas had become, how freedom was spreading across Europe, and how these changes already affected France and would probably affect other places too—all because of the American Revolution—it’s strange that these big changes came from the bad choices or lack of understanding by the British government.

Dr. Franklin tried everything he could to convince the British leaders to change their ways. He talked to them in private and wrote letters explaining that their treatment of America was unfair and unwise.
