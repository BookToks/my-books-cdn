The rest of the unlucky Indians, who had escaped because they were not there, were taken to Lancaster and put in jail to keep them safe.

The governor made a public announcement, saying he strongly disapproved of what happened. He offered a reward to find the people who did it and said no one should hurt peaceful Indians again.
