If this plan is executed, and succeeds, as projected, without interruption for one hundred years, the sum will be then one hundred and thirty-one thousand pounds ; of which I would have the managers of the donation to the town of Boston then lay out, at their discretion, one undred thousand pounds in public works, which may be judged of most general utility to the inhabitants Buch as fortifications, bridges, aqueducts, public buildings, baths, pavements, or whatever may make living in the town more convenient to its people, and render it more agreeable to strangers resorting thither for health, or a temporary residence.
