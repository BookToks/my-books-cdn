On War.

This is a translation of the book called "Vom Kriege."

It includes a list of sources and an index.

The book is about military skills and science, and about war. It was worked on by Michael Eliot Howard and Peter Paret.

The book was first printed as a paperback in 1989 by Princeton University Press. It is made with special acid-free paper to last a long time. It was printed in the United States.
