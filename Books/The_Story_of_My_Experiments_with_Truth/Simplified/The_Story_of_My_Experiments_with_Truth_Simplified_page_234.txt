After a baby is born, it copies what its parents do and depends on them for many years to grow.

Couples who understand this don’t have sex just to satisfy their desires, but only when they want to have children. I think it’s very wrong to believe that sex is something you must do all the time, like sleeping or eating.

The world continues because people have children, and since the world is God’s playground and shows His greatness, having children should be done carefully to keep things in order. Someone who understands this will control their desires, learn what is needed for the health and happiness of their children, and pass that knowledge on to future generations.
