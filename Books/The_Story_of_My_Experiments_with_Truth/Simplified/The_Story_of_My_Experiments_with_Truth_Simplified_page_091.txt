I must have written and rewritten it many times. But it made me feel lighter, like a heavy weight was lifted off me. Almost right away, she replied something like this: I got your honest letter. We both felt happy and laughed a lot about it.

The lie you said you told is okay. But it’s good that you told us the real truth. My invitation still stands, and we’re definitely expecting you next Sunday. We can’t wait to hear all about your child marriage and have fun joking with you.

Do I need to tell you that our friendship is not affected at all by this?

So, I got rid of the problem of lying, and from then on, I never hesitated to talk about being married when I needed to.
