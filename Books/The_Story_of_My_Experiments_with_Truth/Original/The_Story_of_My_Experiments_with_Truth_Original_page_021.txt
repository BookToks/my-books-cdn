For it is unbroken torture to me that I am still so far from Him, who, as I fully know, governs every breadth of my life, and Whose offspring I am. I know that it is the evil passions within that keep me so far from Him, and yet I cannot get away from them.
 
 But is must close. I can only take up the actual story in the next chapter.
 
 M. K. GANDHI The Ashram, Sabarmati 26th November, 1925 1. Literally freedom from birth and death. The nearest English equivalent is salvation.
