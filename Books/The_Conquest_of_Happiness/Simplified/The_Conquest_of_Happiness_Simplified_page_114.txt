Right now, among educated people, men’s sex lives are more confused and filled with feelings of guilt than women’s.

More and more people are starting to realize, even if the government doesn’t, that traditional sex education for young kids is harmful. The best rule is simple: don’t teach kids about sexual right and wrong until they are almost teenagers, and don’t make them think anything natural about their bodies is gross.

When it’s time to talk about morals, make sure the lessons make sense and you can explain why. But this book isn’t about education. It’s about what adults can do to reduce the bad effects of bad teaching that makes people feel guilty for no good reason.
