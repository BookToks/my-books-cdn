But if she really wants to have a baby, she feels forced to get married, and almost definitely loses her job. She ends up living with much less comfort than before because her husband probably doesn’t earn more money than she did, and now he has to support a whole family, not just one person.

After being independent, she feels annoyed to have to rely on someone else for every bit of money. Because of all this, many women don’t want to become mothers.

If a woman does decide to have a baby, she faces a new and big problem that women in the past didn’t have: there aren’t many good helpers for housework. Because of this, she gets stuck at home, forced to do many small chores that don’t match her skills or education. If she doesn’t do these chores herself, she gets frustrated and angry at the helpers who don’t do them right.
