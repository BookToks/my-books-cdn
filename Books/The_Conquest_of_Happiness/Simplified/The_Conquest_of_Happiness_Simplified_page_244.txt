I don’t think there’s any reason why parents can’t make their kids happier. But to do this, like in all equal relationships that people want today, you need to be gentle and caring. You also need to respect the other person, which isn’t easy because everyday life can be tough and aggressive.

Let’s think about the happiness of being a parent. First, in a natural, biological way. Then, as something deeper that happens when a parent truly respects and cares for others, which is important for a world that believes everyone should be equal.

The basic joy of being a parent comes from two things. One is feeling like part of your own body is living outside of you, continuing even after you’re gone. That part might even keep going by passing on its own life, helping life go on forever.
