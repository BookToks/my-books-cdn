Do you think this is the reward for all the times you waited patiently? But from his side, your behavior looks just like how his behavior looks to you. He doesn’t know about the times you stayed quiet; he only sees the one hundred times you spoke up.

If we could magically read each other’s thoughts, I think most friendships would end at first. But then something good might happen. We would realize a world with no friends is impossible to live in. So, we would learn to like each other even though we know no one is perfect.

We know our friends have flaws, but we still like them overall. What we can’t stand is the idea that they might see us the same way. We want them to believe that, unlike everyone else, we have no faults.
