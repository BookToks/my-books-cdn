The examples we talked about are the hardest to accept. There are other situations where it’s easier to be okay with things. These are times when only small goals get blocked, but the big goals in life still seem possible.

For example, a person doing important work shows a lack of good acceptance if he gets upset because of problems in his love life. If his work really keeps him busy, he should treat these small problems like a rainy day—annoying, but not worth getting upset over.

Some people can’t be patient even with these small problems, and if they let those problems take over, they end up making up a big part of their life.
