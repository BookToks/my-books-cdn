Think about whether the world is actually better because of the moral lessons young people usually get. A lot of these lessons are based on silly superstitions. While many made-up dangers were warned against with strange rules, the real dangers adults face were mostly ignored.

What are the harmful things that an average person might do? Cheating in business without breaking the law, being mean to workers, hurting family members, being nasty to competitors, and fighting fiercely in politics. These bad actions are common among people who seem respectable.

These sins cause pain to the people around them and help ruin society. But these are not the things that make a man feel like he’s lost all hope or that he’s rejected by God when he’s sick.
