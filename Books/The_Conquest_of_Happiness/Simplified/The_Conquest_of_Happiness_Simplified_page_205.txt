To overcome these challenges and feel really excited about life, a person needs to be healthy and full of energy. Or, if they’re lucky, they need to have work that they enjoy just for its own sake.

Health has been getting better in all developed countries over the last hundred years, according to statistics. But energy is harder to measure, and I’m not sure if people are as physically strong now as they used to be.

This is mostly a social problem, so I won’t talk about it much here. But it also has a personal and mental side, which we already talked about when discussing tiredness.

Some people keep their excitement for life even with the problems of modern living. Many others could do the same if they didn’t have inner mental struggles that use up a lot of their energy.
