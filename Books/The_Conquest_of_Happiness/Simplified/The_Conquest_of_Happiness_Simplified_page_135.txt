In the past, mistresses used to care about their servants’ behavior and tried to protect their morals. But nowadays, the problem with servants is so serious that this kind way of looking after maids is less common.

The same thing happens in high-level politics. A leader who takes all the power to achieve big, important goals is surprised when people start to dislike him. He never thinks that his work might have other reasons, or that he might enjoy being in control. 

The speeches and articles he hears often make him believe these ideas are true, so he confuses political talk for real reasons behind people’s actions.
