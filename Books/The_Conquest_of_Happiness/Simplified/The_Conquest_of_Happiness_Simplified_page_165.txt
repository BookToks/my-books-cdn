Part II: Causes of happiness  
Chapter 10: Is happiness still possible?

People often wonder if being happy is still something we can achieve today. Life can be really stressful, and many challenges make it hard to feel truly joyful.

But happiness is still possible. It might look different for each person, and it doesn’t mean you have to be happy all the time.

Finding happiness means understanding what matters most to you and focusing on those things. It’s about enjoying the small moments and being grateful for what you have.

Even when life gets tough, you can still find reasons to smile and feel good inside. So yes, happiness is still possible if you try to notice the good in your life.
