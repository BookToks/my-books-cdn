Because of all this, when a person finally becomes successful, they are already very stressed and nervous. They are so used to feeling anxious that they can't stop, even when they don’t need to be anymore.  

It’s true that some rich kids don’t have to worry as much, but they usually find ways to stress themselves out anyway. They might gamble or make their parents upset, or stay up late having fun and ruin their health. By the time they grow up, they are just as unhappy as their parents were.  

Most people today, whether by choice or not, live very stressful lives. They are often so tired that they can’t enjoy themselves unless they drink alcohol.  

Putting aside the rich people who are just careless, let’s think about the more common situation of people who get tired from working hard to make a living.
