This causes them to get into different kinds of trouble, and since their unconscious mind still believes in old rules, these troubles make them feel sad, regretful, and doubtful. I don’t think many people go through this, but those who do are often the loudest about it.

If you compare the average well-off young people today to those from the Victorian times, you would see that there is now a lot more happiness and real belief in love than there was sixty years ago. The reason some people become cynical is because the old ideas still control their unconscious minds, and there isn’t a clear set of modern rules to guide how people behave today.

The solution isn’t to just feel sorry or long for the past, but to bravely accept today’s way of thinking and work hard to get rid of old superstitions that still hide in people’s minds.
