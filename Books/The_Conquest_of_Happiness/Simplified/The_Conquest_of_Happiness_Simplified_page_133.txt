So far, what he says seems to be true.

But his harsh responses have made him believe that powerful people only care about hiding the bad things they’ve done to stay in power. Cases like this are hard to understand because there is some truth in their view. What has affected them personally feels more important than many other things they haven’t experienced. This makes them see things the wrong way and think that unusual facts are more normal than they really are.

Another common person who feels persecuted is a certain kind of helper who always tries to do good things for others, even when they don’t want help. They get shocked and upset when people don’t thank them. Usually, our reasons for helping others aren’t as pure as we think.
