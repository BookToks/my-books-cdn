Darwin traveled all around the world, but after that, he spent the rest of his life quietly at home. Marx, after causing some revolutions, chose to spend his later years in the British Museum. Overall, you’ll see that great people usually live calm lives, and their fun isn’t the kind that looks exciting to others.

Big achievements need a lot of hard work that takes up most of someone’s energy. So, they don’t have much time or energy for wild fun, except for activities that help them recover, like climbing mountains during holidays.

Being able to handle a life that can be pretty boring is something kids should learn early on.

Today’s parents often make a mistake by giving their kids too many easy entertainments, like shows and tasty treats. They don’t understand how important it is for kids to have days that feel mostly the same, except for special occasions.
