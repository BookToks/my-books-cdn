Your parents’ arguments should not be a good enough reason to give up. If you go ahead with your plan despite what they say, they will change their minds sooner than you or they expect. But if professionals tell you it’s a bad idea, that’s different because beginners should always listen to experts.

I think, in general, people listen too much to what others think, both about big and small things. You should respect public opinion enough to avoid starving or going to jail, but anything more than that is just giving in to unnecessary control. This can mess with your happiness in many ways. For example, think about how you spend money.
