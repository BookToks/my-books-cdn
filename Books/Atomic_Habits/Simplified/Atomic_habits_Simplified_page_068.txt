Chapter Summary The 1st Law of Behavior Change is to make it obvious.

The two most common clues that remind you to do something are time and place.

Creating an implementation intention is a way to link a new habit with a specific time and place.

The implementation intention formula is: I will [BEHAVIOR] at [TIME] in [LOCATION].

Habit stacking is a way to link a new habit with a habit you already have.

The habit stacking formula is: After [CURRENT HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT].
