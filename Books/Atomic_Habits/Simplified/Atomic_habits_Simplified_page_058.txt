Chapter Summary

If you practice enough, your brain will start to notice signs that show what will happen next without you even thinking about it.

When our habits become automatic, we stop paying attention to what we are doing.

Changing behavior always starts with being aware. You need to notice your habits before you can change them.

Pointing-and-Calling helps you become more aware by saying out loud what you are doing.

The Habits Scorecard is an easy activity you can use to understand your behavior better.
