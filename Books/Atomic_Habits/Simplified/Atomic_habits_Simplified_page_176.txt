Chapter Summary The secret to having the best chance at success is to pick the right area to focus on.

Choose the right habit, and it’s easy to get better. Choose the wrong habit, and life feels hard.

Your genes can’t be changed easily. This means they can help you a lot in good situations but can make things harder in bad ones.

Habits are easier to build when they match what you’re naturally good at. Pick habits that fit you best.

Play a game that uses your strengths. If you can’t find one, make your own game.

Genes don’t mean you don’t have to work hard. They just show you what to work hard on.
