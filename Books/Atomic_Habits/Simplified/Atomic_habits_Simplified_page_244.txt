Dopamine isn’t the only chemical that affects your habits. Every action you do involves different parts of your brain and many chemicals. So, if someone says “habits are only about dopamine,” they are leaving out a lot of important stuff.

Dopamine is just one of the key players in how habits form. But in this chapter, I will focus on the dopamine system because it helps explain the science behind wanting, craving, and motivation that drive every habit.
