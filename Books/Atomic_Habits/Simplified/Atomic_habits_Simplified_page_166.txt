1.5: Cut down on things that remind you of your bad habits. Take away anything that makes you want to do them.

Make it less appealing: Change how you think. Focus on the good stuff that happens when you avoid your bad habits.

Make it harder: Add extra steps between you and your bad habits so it’s not easy to do them.

Use a commitment device: Limit your future choices to only the ones that help you.

Make it less satisfying: Find someone to keep you accountable. Ask a friend to watch what you do.

Make a habit contract: Make your bad habits costly and public so it’s painful to keep doing them.
