Chapter Summary The Goldilocks Rule says that people feel most motivated when they work on tasks that are just a little bit hard for them.

The biggest problem for success isn’t failing, it’s getting bored.

When habits become normal and easy, they stop being fun and exciting. That’s when we get bored.

Anyone can work hard when they feel excited. The real skill is to keep going even when the work is not fun.

Experts follow their plan no matter what, but beginners let life distract them.
