The secret to making good habits and stopping bad ones is to know some basic rules about how habits work and how to change them to fit you. Any goal will fail if it goes against how people naturally act.

Your habits are formed by the systems you have in your life. In the next chapters, we will talk about these rules one at a time and show you how to build a system where good habits happen easily and bad habits fade away.
