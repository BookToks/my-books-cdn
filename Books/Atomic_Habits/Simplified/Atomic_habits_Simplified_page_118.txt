Chapter Summary: The 3rd Law of Behavior Change is to make it easy.

The best way to learn is by practicing, not just planning.

Focus on doing something, not just thinking about doing it.

Habit formation means a behavior becomes easier and more automatic the more you repeat it.

How long you’ve been doing a habit doesn’t matter as much as how many times you’ve actually done it.
