FIGURE 7: Habit stacking helps you keep a habit by adding a new action right after something you already do. You can keep doing this to link many habits together, where each one reminds you to do the next.

Your morning habit stack could be like this: 1. After I pour my morning coffee, I will meditate for sixty seconds.

2. After I meditate for sixty seconds, I will write my to-do list for the day.

3. After I write my to-do list, I will start my first task right away.
