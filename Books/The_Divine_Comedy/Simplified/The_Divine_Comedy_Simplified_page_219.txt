“Raphaelmaiamechizabialmi,” Began to shout the fierce mouth, which wasn’t really fitting for sweeter songs.

And then my Guide said to me: “Soul, don’t be stupid, hold onto your courage, and deal with your anger or other strong feelings.

Look around your neck, and you’ll find the belt that keeps it tight. Confused soul, see it there, where it holds your strong chest.”

Then he said to me: “He blames himself; this is Nimrod, whose bad ideas made it so one language in the world stopped being used.

Let’s leave him and not waste words; because to him, every language is like the others, and no one understands them.”

So we traveled a longer way, turned left, and after a short distance, we found another giant who was even fiercer and bigger.
