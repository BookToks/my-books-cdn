Wake up now, and with your fancy words,  
Help him with what he needs so I can feel better.  

I am Beatrice, who obeyed his wishes;  
I come from where I want to go back to;  
Love made me come, and it forces me to speak.  

When I am before my Lord,  
I will often praise you to him.”
