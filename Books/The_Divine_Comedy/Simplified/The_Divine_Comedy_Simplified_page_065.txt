They secretly wished to talk to them.

After a little while, they stopped being so angry and said, “Are you coming alone? Then he should leave—he’s the one who dared to enter this place.

Let him try to go back alone on his crazy path; see if he can. Because you will stay here, since you brought him through such dark places.” 

Think about it, Reader, I felt really upset when I heard those awful words. I believed I would never come back here again.

“I’m begging you, my dear Guide, who has saved me more than seven times and pulled me out of danger many times before, don’t leave me now,” I said. “If we can’t go any farther, then—”
