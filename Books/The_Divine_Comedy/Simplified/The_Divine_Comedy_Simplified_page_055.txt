That thing breaks itself on whatever it hits. So here, the people have to dance their circle dance.

He saw lots of people, more than anywhere else, on both sides, making loud howling noises. They pushed heavy things forward using their chests.

They bumped into each other, and then at that moment, each one turned back, rolling backward, shouting, “Why do you keep going?” and, “Why do you waste your time?” Then they went back around the strange circle to the opposite side, shouting their shame over and over.
