“In all the questions you ask, you really please me,” he said; “but boiling the red water might solve one of the problems you make.

You will see Lethe, but outside this moat, where the souls go to wash themselves when they have taken away their sins.” Then he said: “It is time now to leave the woods; be careful to follow me; stay away from the burning edges, and over all of them the smoke is gone.”
