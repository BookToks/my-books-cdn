If someone truly regrets what they did, they can’t be forgiven right away. And they can’t both regret and want to keep doing it at the same time, because that doesn’t make sense.

Poor me! I was so scared when he grabbed me and said, “Maybe you didn’t think I was a smart person!” He took me to Minos, who wrapped his tail around sinners eight times depending on how bad they are. Minos bit his tail angrily and said, “This thief is to blame.” So now I’m stuck here, and I’m very sad about it.

After telling me this, the flame left, crying and moving its sharp horn wildly.

Then my guide and I moved forward, climbing above another arch covered by a moat. Here, people who cause fights have to pay a fee for their actions.
