We were alone and not afraid at all.

Many times we looked at each other while reading, and it made us lose our color and feel weak. But only once did something really strong happen to us.

When I finally got the smile I had been waiting for, from such a wonderful lover’s kiss, the one who will never be separated from me, kissed me on the mouth while my heart was beating fast.

The book was called Galeotto, and he was the one who wrote it.

That day, we didn’t read any further. All the time, one of us was thinking this, and the other was crying so much, out of pity, that I almost fainted like I was dying and fell down just like a dead body.
