 Wecameuntoanoblecastle’sfoot,57 Seventimesencompassedwithloftywalls, Defendedroundbyafairrivulet; Thiswepassedoverevenasfirmground; ThroughportalssevenIenteredwiththesesages Wecameintoameadowoffreshverdure.
 
 Peopleweretherewithsolemneyesandslow, Ofgreatauthorityintheircountenance; Theyspakebutseldom,andwithgentlevoices.
 
 Thuswewithdrewourselvesupononeside Intoanopeningluminousandlofty, Sothattheyallofthemwerevisible.
 
 Thereopposite,uponthegreenenamel, Werepointedouttomethemightyspirits, WhomtohaveseenIfeelmyselfexalted.
