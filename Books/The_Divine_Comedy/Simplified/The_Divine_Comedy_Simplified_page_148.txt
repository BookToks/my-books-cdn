From Offourbridge, he said: “O Malebranche...”

He swims differently than in the Serchio River.

So, if you don’t want to be caught by our rakes, don’t lift yourself above the pitch.

They grabbed him with more than a hundred rakes.

They said, “You must dance covered, so if you can, you might steal secretly.”

Cooks don’t treat their helpers any differently.

They dunk the meat with hooks into the middle of the pot so it won’t float.

The good Master said to me: “Don’t let it be obvious that you are here. Crouch down behind a rock so you have some cover.

And don’t be afraid of what they do to me, because I know these things.

I’ve been in this kind of trouble before.”
