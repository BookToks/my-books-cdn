14 165. By oneself is evil done; by oneself is one defiled.
 
 By oneself is evil left undone; by oneself is one made pure. Purity and impurity depended on oneself; no one can purify another.
 
 166. Let one not neglect one’s own welfare for the sake of another, however great. Clearly understanding one’s own welfare, let one be intent upon the good.
