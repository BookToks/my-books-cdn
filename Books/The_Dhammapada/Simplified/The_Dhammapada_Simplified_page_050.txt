165. You do bad things by yourself, and that can make you feel dirty inside.  

But you can also choose not to do bad things, and that helps you feel clean and good.  

Whether you feel pure or dirty depends on you; no one else can make you pure.  

166. Don’t forget to take care of yourself, even if you want to help someone else a lot.  

Know what’s best for you, and focus on doing good things.
