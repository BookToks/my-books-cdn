The Dhammapada: The Buddha's Path of Wisdom

The Dhammapada is a book that shares the teachings of the Buddha. It helps people understand how to live a good and peaceful life.

This version was translated by Acharya Buddharakkhita. 

You can learn more about it from the Buddha Dharma Education Association. They have a website at www.buddhanet.net and an email bdea@buddhanet.net.
