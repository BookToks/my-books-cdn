The smell of tagara and sandal is faint, but the good people have a wonderful presence that even the gods notice.

Mara, the evil one, can never trick those who are truly good. These people stay careful and gain perfect understanding.

A beautiful lotus flower grows on a pile of trash by the roadside, smelling nice and looking lovely.

In the same way, a student of the greatest teacher shines with wisdom, even among people who are confused and lost.
