Some people know the truth inside them, but they don’t say it out loud.  

For these people, their spirit lives quietly and in a steady rhythm.  

When you see your friend on the street or at the market, let your inner spirit guide your words.  

Let the true voice inside you speak softly to your friend’s ear. Their soul will remember the truth in your heart, like how the taste of wine stays even after the color is gone and the cup is empty.  

An astronomer once asked, “Master, what about Time?”
