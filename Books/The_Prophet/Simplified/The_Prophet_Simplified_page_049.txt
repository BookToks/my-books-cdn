He said: Your clothes cover a lot of your beauty, but they don’t hide what’s not beautiful.

Even though you wear clothes to have privacy, they can also feel like chains holding you back.

I wish you could feel the sun and wind on more of your skin and wear less clothing, because life is in sunlight and wind.

Some of you say, "The north wind made the clothes we wear." And I say, yes, it was the north wind, but shame was the tool he used, and weakness was the thread.

When he finished, he laughed in the forest.

Remember, modesty is like a shield to protect you from dirty eyes.
