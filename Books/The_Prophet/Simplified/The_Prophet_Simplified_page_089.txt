It is your desire inside us that wants things.

It is your push inside us that tries to change our nights, which belong to you, into days that also belong to you.

We can’t ask you for anything because you already know what we need before we even realize it. You are our need; and when you give us more of yourself, you give us everything.

Then a hermit, who visited the city once a year, came forward and said, “Talk to us about Pleasure.”
