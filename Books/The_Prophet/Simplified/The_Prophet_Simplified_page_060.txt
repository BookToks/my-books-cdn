He said: You love making rules, but you love breaking them even more.

It's like kids playing by the sea who build sandcastles carefully and then knock them down while laughing.

While you build your sandcastles, the ocean brings more sand to the shore, and when you break them, the ocean laughs with you.

The ocean always laughs with people who are innocent.

But what about those who don’t see life as an ocean, and don’t think laws are just sandcastles? Instead, they see life as a rock, and laws as tools to shape it how they want.

What about the person who can’t walk but hates dancers?
