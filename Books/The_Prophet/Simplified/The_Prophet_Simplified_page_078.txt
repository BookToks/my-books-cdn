He said: You start talking when you stop feeling calm inside. When you can’t be alone with your own thoughts, you live through your words, and making noise becomes a way to pass time.

When you talk a lot, your thinking gets messed up.

Thinking is like a bird that needs space. Words can open its wings a little, but they don’t let it fly free.

Some people talk a lot because they are scared to be alone.

Being alone and quiet shows them who they really are, and they want to avoid that.

There are also people who talk without really knowing or thinking, and they end up sharing things they don’t fully understand.
