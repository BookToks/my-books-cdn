But now our sleep is gone and our dream is finished, and it is no longer morning.

The midday has come, and our half-awake state is now full daylight, so we have to say goodbye.

If we meet again in the fading memories, we will talk together once more, and you will sing me a deeper song.

And if our hands meet in another dream, we will build another tower up in the sky.

After saying this, he signaled to the sailors, and they quickly pulled up the anchor and set the ship free from where it was tied, moving east.

A shout came from the people like one big heart, rising into the evening and carrying out over the sea like a loud trumpet.

Only Almitra stayed quiet, watching after them.
