They said these things in their quiet moments; but if they were even quieter inside, they would have known that I was only looking for the secret of your happiness and pain, and I was chasing the bigger parts of you that fly in the sky.

But the hunter was also the one being hunted; because many of my arrows left my bow only to hit my own chest.

And the flyer was also the crawler; because when my wings spread in the sun, their shadow on the ground looked like a turtle.

And I, the believer, was also the doubter; because often I put my finger in my own wound to believe in you more and understand you better.
