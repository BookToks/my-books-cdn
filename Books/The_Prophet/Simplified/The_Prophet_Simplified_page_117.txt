You shouldn’t be sad for ever being blind or sorry for ever being deaf.

Because one day, you will understand the secret reasons behind everything. And you will appreciate darkness just like you appreciate light.

After saying this, he looked around and saw the pilot of his ship standing by the steering wheel, looking at the full sails and then at the faraway horizon.

He said, “The captain of my ship is very patient, even more than patient.”

The wind blows, and the sails are restless; even the rudder wants guidance. But quietly, my captain waits for my silence.

And these sailors, who have listened to the big sea’s song, have also listened to me patiently.

Now, they won’t have to wait any longer.
