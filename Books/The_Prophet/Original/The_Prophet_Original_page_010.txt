And in the twelfth year, on the sevet1th day of Ielool, the month of reaping, he climbed the hill without the city walls and looked seaward; and he beheld his ship coming with the mist.
 
 Then the gates of his heart were flung open, and his joy flew far over the sea.
 
 And he closed his eyes and prayed in the silences of his soul.
 
 But as he descended the hill, a sadness came upon him, and he thought in his heart: How shall I go in peace and without sorrow'? Nay, not without a wound in the spirit shall I leave this city.
