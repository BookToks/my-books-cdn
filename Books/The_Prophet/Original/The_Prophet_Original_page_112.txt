Some of you have deemed me proud and over-shy to receive gifts.
 
 I Too proud indeed am to receive wages, but not gifts.
 
 I And though have eaten berries among the hills when you would have had me sit at your board, And slept in the portico of the temple when you would gladly have sheltered me, Yet was it not your loving mindfulness of my days and my nights that made food sweet to my mouth and girdled my sleep with visions'?
 
 For this I bless you most: You give much and know not that you give at all.
