And Almit ra the seeress said, Blessed be this day and this place and your spirit that has spoken.
 
 And he answered, Was it I who spoke'? Was I not also a listener'?
 
 Then he descended the steps of the Temple and all the people followed him.
 
 And he reached his ship and stood upon the deck.
 
 And facing the people again, he raised his voice and said: People of Orphalese, the wind bids me leave you.
 
 Less hasty am I than the wind, yet I must go.
 
 We wanderers, ever seeking the lonelier way, begin no day where we have ended another day; and no sunrise finds us where sunset left us.
