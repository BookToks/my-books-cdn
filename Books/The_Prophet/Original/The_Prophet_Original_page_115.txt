And 1t 1s with this belief and this knowledge that I say, You are not enclosed within your bodies, nor confined to houses or fields.
 
 That which is you dwells above the mountain and roves with the wind.
 
 It is not a thing that crawls into the sun for warmth or digs holes into darkness for safety, But a thing free, a spirit that envelops the earth and moves in the ether.
 
 If these be vague words, then seek not to clear them.
 
 Vague and nebulous is the beginning of all things, but not their end, And I fain would have you remember me as a beginning.
 
 Life, and all that lives, is conceived in the mist and not in the crystal.
